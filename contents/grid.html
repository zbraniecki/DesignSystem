Grid

When all of your measurements follow the same rules, you automatically get a more consistent UI.

Space

Grid minimum space is 4px. This unit helps control the proportion, balance and vertical alignment of every Firefox components. All elements, including icons, ared based on this unit.

Workflow tip

You can use Nudg.it to set your keyboard increment by 4px in Sketch.

Alignment

When you design a component, elements align following the grid. The same principle applies when you stack multiple elements within and between a component.
